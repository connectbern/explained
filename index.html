Understanding Our Projects

We operate two interconnected initiatives to achieve our vision of a more connected society

🌟 Connect Bern - The Main Project

Connect Bern is our primary initiative - a physical and social space where anyone can come to experience genuine human connection.

What we offer:

  • A welcoming space near Wankdorf where socializing is the primary goal
  • Daily activities and events focused on bringing people together
  • WhatsApp community groups for ongoing connection
  • No commercial interests or hidden agendas - just people being people

Perfect for: Anyone seeking connection, locals wanting to meet new people, travelers, or simply those who want to combat loneliness

🏠 Staying in Bern - The Volunteer Project

Staying in Bern is our intensive volunteer program designed for those who want to deeply engage with and help realize our vision.

What makes it special:

  • Long-term volunteers (2+ weeks) who actively help build the community
  • Shared sleeping space creating "permanent international sleepover vibes"
  • Morning exercises and community rituals
  • An intense, transformative experience of communal living

This volunteer program is crucial for maintaining the energy and spirit of Connect Bern, creating the crowd and context that makes our mission possible

Breaking the Barriers of Modern Isolation

🎯 Our Mission

We're creating a society where human connection is the priority. Where you can walk into any space and feel welcome to talk to anyone. Where saying "hi" to a stranger isn't weird, but wonderful.

🔮 Our Vision

Starting in Bern, we're building physical spaces and communities where anyone can come, be themselves, and connect with others. No hidden agendas, no commercial interests - just pure human connection.

🌟 Our Goals

Create events that bring people together, foster a spirit of inclusion and joy, and build a network of spaces where loneliness has no place and everyone feels they belong.

Creating a Safe Space for All

We are deeply committed to creating an environment where everyone feels safe, respected, and valued. This isn't just a principle - it's a fundamental requirement that we actively work on every single day.

🛡️ Zero Tolerance

We have zero tolerance for violence, harassment, or any form of discrimination. Continuous non-consensual behavior is taken seriously and addressed immediately.

🤝 Active Protection

We actively protect vulnerable groups, especially women, LGBTQ+ individuals, people of color, and minorities. Their safety and comfort is our priority.

👂 Victim-First Approach

When conflicts arise, we prioritize the needs and safety of those who feel uncomfortable or threatened. We listen, we believe, and we act.

📢 Clear Communication

Signs, guidelines, and open discussions ensure everyone understands our boundaries. We're not afraid to address uncomfortable topics to maintain safety.

🌈 Inclusive by Design

Everyone is welcome, but if someone makes others feel unsafe for unjustified reasons, we address it immediately and decisively.

💬 Women-Only Spaces

We maintain dedicated women-only groups and spaces, recognizing the need for additional safe environments within our community.

"Making everyone feel comfortable means being aware that not everyone starts from the same place. We work actively to level the playing field and ensure that Connect Bern is a space where humanity can truly connect - safely, respectfully, and joyfully."
